## Vision

Voice dictation is broken. Built-in dictation quality is declining, Dragon costs $699, and Wispr Flow had a privacy scandal. Dictaro bridges the gap with local Whisper processing and intelligent AI cleanup — understanding what you *meant* to say, not just what you said.

## The Problem

Apple's dictation quality thread has 509 "Me Too" responses. The tools that work are either prohibitively expensive (Dragon at $699), privacy nightmares (cloud-processed voice data), platform-locked (Superwhisper is macOS only), or dumb (Voice In has 600K users but no AI intelligence).

## Key Differentiators

- **Privacy-first architecture**: All processing happens on-device using Whisper. Zero data leaves your machine, ever
- **Intelligent cleanup**: Not just transcription — AI understands context, removes filler, fixes grammar, and formats for the target medium
- **Cross-platform**: macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, Android — one subscription, all platforms
